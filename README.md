# 🛍️ Customer Shopping Behavior Analysis

An end-to-end **Data Analytics project** that analyzes customer shopping behavior using retail transaction data.  
This project demonstrates how raw data can be transformed into **actionable business insights** using **Python, SQL, and Power BI**.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

Retail companies generate large amounts of customer transaction data.  
This project analyzes **3,900 customer purchases** to uncover patterns in spending behavior, product preferences, and customer loyalty.

The objective is to simulate a **real-world data analytics workflow** from data preparation to business insights.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Retail companies want to better understand their customers in order to:

- Improve **sales performance**
- Increase **customer satisfaction**
- Strengthen **customer loyalty**
- Optimize **marketing and product strategies**

This project answers the key question:

**How can consumer shopping data be used to identify trends and improve business decision-making?**

---

# 📊 Dataset Summary

The dataset contains customer demographics, purchase behavior, and transaction details.

**Dataset Information**

- 📦 Total Purchases: **3,900**
- 📊 Columns: **18**
- ⚠️ Missing Values: **37 values in the Review Rating column**

**Key Features**

- Customer demographics (Age, Gender, Location)
- Purchase information (Item Purchased, Category, Purchase Amount)
- Shopping behavior (Discount Applied, Previous Purchases, Review Rating)
- Logistics information (Shipping Type)

---

# ⚙️ Project Workflow

## 🐍 Data Preparation (Python)

- Loaded dataset using **Pandas**
- Performed **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**
- Handled missing values
- Standardized column names
- Created new features such as **Age Groups**
- Prepared data for database analysis

---

## 🗄️ Data Analysis (SQL)

SQL queries were written to answer important business questions such as:

- 💰 Revenue generated by male vs female customers
- 🛒 Customers who used discounts but still spent above average
- ⭐ Top-rated products based on customer reviews
- 🚚 Spending comparison between **Standard vs Express shipping**
- 👥 Customer segmentation (New, Returning, Loyal)
- 🔁 Relationship between repeat buyers and subscriptions
- 👨‍👩‍👧 Revenue contribution by different age groups

---

## 📈 Data Visualization (Power BI)

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** was created to present insights visually.

The dashboard includes:

- 👥 Customer distribution by subscription status
- 💰 Revenue by product category
- 📊 Sales distribution across age groups
- ⭐ Average review ratings
- 🛍️ Purchase behavior insights

---

# 🔑 Key Insights

- 👩 Female customers generate slightly higher revenue than male customers
- 🚚 Customers using **Express Shipping** spend more per transaction
- 🔁 Loyal customers contribute a large portion of total revenue
- 💸 Some products rely heavily on **discount-based purchases**
- 👨‍👩‍👧 Younger and middle-aged customers contribute a significant share of revenue

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis:

- 🎯 Promote **subscription programs** to increase customer retention
- ⭐ Highlight **top-rated products** in marketing campaigns
- 🎁 Optimize **discount strategies** to balance sales and profitability
- 🧠 Focus marketing on **high-value customer segments**
- 🔁 Convert **new customers into returning and loyal customers**
